FAQ

The objectives of the Research Excellence Program (Research grants)

Funding research projects submitted by researchers from research institutes or local universities. The program will consider proposals for joint research from local and international researchers (including Uzbek scientists abroad), proposals for joint research from researchers and the private sector, and proposals from young scientists.

The maximum period for the implementation of projects under grants allocated under the Research Excellence Program (Research grants)

The project implementation period is up to 2 years (24 months). As an exception, the duration of the project may be extended if the stated basis is deemed appropriate.

Through which website (portal) are applications accepted?

Applications are accepted on the website https://wbgrants.mininnovation.uz/ Applicants will have the opportunity to submit their applications after proper registration process.

Expenses are allowed during the project implementation:

- Wage; - Purchase of equipment; - Travel expenses; - Laboratory materials and equipment; - Protection of intellectual property and publications; - Third party services; - Other expenses that do not interfere with the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The language of submission

The project proposal must be written and submitted in English. In addition to the English version, an additional Uzbek or Russian version may be provided. Due to the international nature of the project appraisal process, the English version is accepted as the main proposal.
